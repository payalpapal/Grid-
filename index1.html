Strawberry

Strawberries are typically depicted as bright red, heart-shaped fruits with small, yellow seeds embedded on their surface. They are known for their sweet, juicy taste and pleasant aroma.

Apple

Apples are round, fleshy fruits with red, green, or yellow skin, and firm, white flesh. They are typically 2-4 inches in diameter and vary in size, shape, and acidity depending on the variety.

Mango

A mango is a tropical fruit, typically oval or oblong, with a smooth skin ranging in color from green to yellow or red, and a juicy, sweet, and aromatic pulp surrounding a large, flat pit.

Grapes

Grapes are small, sweet, juicy berries that grow in clusters on vines. They are known for their round shape and are typically green, purple, or red, though other colors exist.
